🚀 QUICK START - Writer Playbook Webhook Setup
================================================================

TOTAL TIME: 15-20 minutes
DIFFICULTY: Beginner-friendly

================================================================
📋 WHAT YOU'LL NEED:
================================================================
1. A computer with internet
2. An email address
3. Your Writer account
4. These project files (you have them!)

================================================================
⚡ SUPER QUICK STEPS:
================================================================

STEP 1: GITHUB (5 min)
  □ Go to github.com
  □ Sign up for free account
  □ Click "New repository"
  □ Name it: writer-webhook-site
  □ Click "Create repository"
  □ Click "Upload files"
  □ Upload ALL files from this folder
  □ Click "Commit changes"

STEP 2: VERCEL (5 min)
  □ Go to vercel.com
  □ Click "Sign up with GitHub"
  □ Click "Import Project"
  □ Select "writer-webhook-site"
  □ Click "Deploy"
  □ Wait 2 minutes
  □ Click "Visit" to see your site
  □ COPY the webhook URL shown on your site

STEP 3: WRITER PLAYBOOK (5 min)
  □ Open your Writer playbook
  □ Add webhook/HTTP action
  □ Paste your Vercel URL
  □ Set method to POST
  □ Run playbook to test

STEP 4: VERIFY (2 min)
  □ Go to vercel.com/dashboard
  □ Click your project
  □ Click "Functions" tab
  □ Check logs for incoming data
